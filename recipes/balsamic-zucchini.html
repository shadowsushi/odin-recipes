Balsamic Grilled Zucchini

Description

A simple grilled zucchini recipe with a touch of balsamic vinegar, garlic, and Italian seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 2 zucchinis
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-low heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Brush zucchini with olive oil. Sprinkle seasoning over zucchini.
  3. Cook on preheated grill 3 to 4 minutes per side.
